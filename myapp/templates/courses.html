NEW OPERATOR


This course has been designed for people having little or no previous forklift experience. The course includes 8 hours of classroom study including practical training. Then we take a theoretical and practical test of forklift operations. This course can be conducted at your premises or at our training centre as per your convenience. This course covers applicable Provincial and Federal legislation and guidelines, liability, documentation and responsibilities.

EXPERIENCED OPERATOR


NKS Forklift Training Center offers the best courses for experienced operators. The operators who wish to renew their certification or upgrade to a higher level are trained by NKS. The best qualified trainers will help you in developing essential new skills that are in demand in market.People can get highly paid jobs in the fork lifting industry after upgrading their forklift certifications. So contact us if you wish to get re-certificated or if you wish to upgrade your licence. The experienced operator courses are facilitated by certified trainers with over 8 years of experience.
